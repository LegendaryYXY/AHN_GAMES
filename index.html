About Games

Welcome to AHN MIND GAMES! We are Aarnav, Nehaj, and Hanan, a team of 6th graders passionate about creating fun, interactive, and brain-stimulating games.

Aarnav loves solving puzzles and designing challenging games that make you think deeply. He brings creativity and innovation to our team, always looking for new ways to engage players.

Nehaj is our coding expert, responsible for bringing our ideas to life with code. He has a keen eye for detail and ensures every game runs smoothly and looks visually appealing.

Hanan is the storyteller of our group, making sure each game has a purpose and feels fun and meaningful. He designs the rules and makes sure everyone enjoys playing!

We created AHN MIND GAMES as part of the Digital Fest, an exciting event where young coders showcase their skills in technology, design, and creativity. Our games are designed to entertain and educate, helping players improve their memory, logic, and problem-solving skills while having fun.

We hope you enjoy playing our games as much as we enjoyed creating them. Thank you for joining us on this journey at the Digital Fest. Let's challenge your mind with AHN Mind Games!
